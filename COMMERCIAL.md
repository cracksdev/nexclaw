# Commercial use, trademarks, and future paid offerings

## Open source license

The code in this repository is released under the **MIT License** unless a file header states otherwise. You may use it for commercial and non-commercial purposes, subject to the conditions in [LICENSE](LICENSE) (including retaining the copyright notice).

## Trademarks

**“NexClaw”** and related logos are branding of the project. The MIT license does **not** grant rights to use those names or marks in a way that suggests the official project endorses your product. Unofficial builds should make clear they are not the official distribution.

## Official builds vs. source builds

The maintainers may publish **signed installers** (e.g. Windows `.exe`, macOS `.dmg`) as the recommended way for end users to install the app. Those builds may include the same MIT-licensed code plus packaging, signing, and update channels. Purchasing or subscribing (if offered in the future) may apply only to **official channels** or **hosted services**, not to the underlying MIT source.

## Future paid tiers (optional)

If paid features or subscriptions are introduced:

- **Grandfathering** (e.g. early adopters keep access) will be defined in product policy and, where applicable, enforced via **accounts or license keys** backed by a server—not by pretending the MIT client cannot be modified.
- Forks that remove payment UI remain permissible under MIT; value in paid offerings typically comes from **convenience, signing, support, sync, or closed components** (open-core), as described in [docs/MONETIZATION_MODEL.md](docs/MONETIZATION_MODEL.md).
